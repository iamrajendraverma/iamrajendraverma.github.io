* **Interval Switch**: Advanced scheduling for total automation.
  * **Once Mode**: Trigger a delayed start after a specific waiting period.
  * **Daily Mode**: Schedule regular ON/OFF cycles at a set time every day. Perfect for irrigation or routine lighting.
